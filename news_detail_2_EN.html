Public Discussion of the Curriculum “Effective Governance in the Context of the European Dimension”

On 27 March 2026, within the framework of the international Erasmus+ project No. 101237607 - EG4HDCEEU4UI - ERASMUS-EDU-2025-CBHE “Good Governance Practice: European Experience for Ukraine”, a public discussion of the developed curriculum “Effective Governance in the Context of the European Dimension” took place. The curriculum is intended for the training of higher education students at Ukrainian partner universities.

Representatives of all partner organisations of the project consortium, as well as invited experts, took part in the event. During the meeting, the structure, content, and key components of the developed curriculum were presented.

The curriculum was presented by the project team of the coordinating university, including Vitalii Stashuk, Nataliia Ridei, and Nataliia Tytova, who introduced the participants to the main objectives, thematic modules, and expected learning outcomes.

During the discussion, participants exchanged views on the content of the curriculum, its compliance with modern requirements for the training of specialists in the field of management and administration, as well as the possibilities for its implementation in the educational process of partner universities.

As a result of the discussion, the curriculum was approved, and further steps for its implementation in the educational activities of Ukrainian higher education institutions — project partners — were identified.
