START OF THE ERASMUS+ CBHE PROJECT “Good Governance Practices: European Experience for Ukraine”

On December 4, 2025, the kick-off meeting of the new Erasmus+ CBHE project “Good Governance Practice: European Experience for Ukraine” (101237607 – EG4HDCEEU4UI – ERASMUS-EDU-2025-CBHE) “Good Governance Practice: European Experience for Ukraine” took place, the coordinator/grantholder of which is Mykhailo Dragomanov Ukrainian State University.

The project consortium partners are Borys Grinchenko Kyiv Metropolitan University, Vasyl Stefanyk Carpathian National University, Mykola Gogol Nizhyn State University, Kryvyi Rih National University, Berdyansk State Pedagogical University, I.F. Kuras Institute of Political and Ethno-National Studies, Baltic International Academy (Latvia), University of Economics in Bratislava (Slovakia), and Marie Curie-Skłodowska University (Poland).

The implementation of the Erasmus+ project is aimed at introducing European approaches to good governance, developing institutional capacity and exchanging best practices that will contribute to the modernization of management processes and sustainable development of Ukraine. The kick-off meeting of the project was an important stage for determining priority areas of activity, agreeing on approaches to the implementation of planned activities and establishing effective cooperation between partners. The project opens up wide opportunities for international partnership, academic mobility and the introduction of European experience, which will have a positive impact on the development of management and educational practices in Ukraine.
