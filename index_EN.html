Project Objectives

1.

To explore the experience of European partners in the field of higher education management and to develop proposals for adapting this experience for implementation within the governance models of Ukrainian higher education institutions.

2.

To provide programme participants with the competencies and awareness necessary for further dissemination of good governance standards.

3.

To promote the experience and standards of good governance as a guarantee of sustainable democracy.

4.

To contribute to the development of local democracy through educational programmes.

5.

To establish communication channels among consortium members in order to facilitate the exchange of successful European practices.

6.

To develop and implement a training course and conduct its pilot testing in seven educational institutions.

7.

To modernise educational programmes of specialised higher education institutions in the field of governance and to integrate European-based good governance standards into national curricula.
